Other stuff

  • Each player gets the option to create a bank account. When first joining the server, as part of the introduction, the player will be teleported to a bank and asked if they would like to create an account. If yes, the player will be walked through the steps and if not the on the introduction will continue. Bank accounts get a small amount of interest added to them every day.
  • Alot of the land will be protected from building or modifying Each player / gang will have to buy a building of their owm or build their own out of town. Buildings can be robbed by either having an insider, or forcing entry (more on that later).
  • Each building you own in the city, the more power you gain. You won't gain power for buildings that are outside of the city.
  • If you own a building, you can apply for permission to rent it to other players who must pay you with in-game money weekly. If they don't pay, the owner can get them evicted.
